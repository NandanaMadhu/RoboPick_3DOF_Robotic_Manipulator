# 🔩 Hardware Specifications

## Controller

- Arduino Uno
- ATmega328P Microcontroller
- 16 MHz Clock Frequency

---

## Servo Motors

### MG996R

- Operating Voltage: 4.8–7.2V
- High Torque Servo
- Metal Gear
- 180° Rotation

### SG90

- Operating Voltage: 4.8–6V
- Lightweight Micro Servo
- Plastic Gear
- 180° Rotation

---

## PWM Driver

PCA9685

- 16 PWM Channels
- I2C Communication
- 12-bit PWM Resolution

---

## Mechanical Structure

- 3 Degrees of Freedom
- Servo-actuated Mechanical Gripper
- Acrylic Robot Arm Frame

---

## Applications

- Pick-and-Place
- Educational Robotics
- Automation Demonstration
- Motion Control Experiments
